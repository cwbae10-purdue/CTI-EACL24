October is National Cyber Security month.
Groups like the National Cyber Security Alliance are promoting awareness of cyber security.

The Secretary will discuss the: • urgent need to counter the threat of cyber attacks • shared responsibility for staying safe online • leadership role DHS is playing on cybersecurity
