As we enter the New Year, be sure to keep up, or adopt, these good data security habits to avoid identity theft
Are you doing enough to reduce the risk of becoming a victim of identity theft?
As more and more of our personal information is online and cybercriminals target everything from bank accounts to tax refunds, identity theft is a growing concern.
As you start the New Year, this is a good time to reflect on what kinds of measures you should take to prevent thieves from stealing and using your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) for fraudulent activities.
With your sensitive information in their hands, crooks can make bogus payments, open new accounts or apply for loans in your name.
