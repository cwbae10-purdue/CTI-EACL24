New malware that attacks point-of-sale (POS) systems had been detected in 40 countries in recent months.
The malware, dubbed “Dexter” is able to skim credit card information from compromised POS systems.
Credit and debit card users across the world have been warned to check statements carefully especially after the holiday season, as details of the malware emerged from researchers in Russia.
So far it has been detected at retailers, restaurants and hotel chains.
