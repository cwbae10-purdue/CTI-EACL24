Since Yesterday evening, the gang behind Nuwar (also called the Storm Worm), have registered a number of blogspot accounts to spread their malware.
Clicking on an image will redirect the browser to an executable called love.exe while clicking on the link in the text below the image will download a file named withlove.exe.
Both executables are variants of Nuwar.
