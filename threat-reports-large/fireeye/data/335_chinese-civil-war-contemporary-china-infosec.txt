In this latest podcast, Richard Bejtlich sat down with Dr. Christopher Lew, author of the book "Historical Dictionary of the Chinese Civil War" to look at lessons learned during this course of time.
According to Lew, trying to interpret current events without understanding this era of Chinese history is like doing so in a complete vacuum.
A lack of context during this historical period of time -- where communism bloomed, the parties split and the People's Republic of China was formed - limits our ability to understand deeper motivations and drivers of Contemporary China.
Learn how this historical approach impacts information security and cyberwar in this latest podcast.
