When it comes to the Internet of Things (IoT), a standard set of security precautions and practices has yet to be developed.
In this podcast, Charles Henderson of IBM’s X-Force Red discusses the present and future of IoT security.
Charles draws on his own experience with connected cars and home automation systems to examine the state of IoT security, particularly as it relates to secondhand devices.
He also offers potential solutions for manufacturers, sellers and consumers.
