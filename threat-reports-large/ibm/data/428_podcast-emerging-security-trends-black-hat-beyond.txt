In this short podcast, IBM Executive Security Advisor Diana Kelley highlights some of the security trends that were on display at Black Hat USA 2016.
Listen in to hear her discuss:
How collaboration and knowledge sharing have matured into security best practices;
Why some companies are still struggling to bring cybersecurity into the boardroom;
What is needed to make threat intelligence data actionable and useful; and
The potential of cognitive security and the benefits of a tool like IBM Watson for Cyber Security.
