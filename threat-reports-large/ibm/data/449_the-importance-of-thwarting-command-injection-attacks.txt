Command injection attacks are among the most common attacks on the Internet, where numerous entry points offer openings for a system compromise.
The chances of success are enhanced by the complexity of measures needed to defend against them.
By taking advantage of vulnerabilities in your operating system or applications, attackers can penetrate critical web servers, issue arbitrary commands and access back-end databases.
